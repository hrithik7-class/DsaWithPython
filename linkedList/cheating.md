### **Key Formulas for Reference**
* **Arithmetic Mean:** $\bar{X} = A + \frac{\sum fd}{N} \times i$
* **Median:** $M = L + \frac{\frac{N}{2} - cf}{f} \times i$
* **Standard Deviation:** $\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum fd^2}{N} - (\frac{\sum fd}{N})^2} \times i$
* **Karl Pearson’s $r$:** $r = \frac{N\sum XY - (\sum X)(\sum Y)}{\sqrt{[N\sum X^2 - (\sum X)^2][N\sum Y^2 - (\sum Y)^2]}}$
* **Spearman’s Rank:** $R = 1 - \frac{6\sum D^2}{N(N^2 - 1)}$
